"SSH keys are not tied to the machine where they were generated. You can back up the private and public key files (typically id_rsa and id_rsa.pub in the ~/.ssh/ directory) and restore them on another computer by copying them to the ~/.ssh/ directory on the new machine. Ensure the private key file has strict permissions (e.g., chmod 600 id_rsa)." (source: Grok)
