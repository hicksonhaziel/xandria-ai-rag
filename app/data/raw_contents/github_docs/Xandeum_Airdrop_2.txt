Token Vesting Schedule
All Airdrop 2 tokens include a vesting schedule to ensure long-term community alignment:

4-month cliff: Tokens cannot be claimed for the first 4 months after distributor funding (~120 days)
12-month continuous linear vesting: After the cliff, tokens vest continuously every minute over 12 months (~365 days)
Claim anytime: You can claim your proportionally vested tokens at any time (not monthly steps)
After 1 day of vesting: claim 1/365th of your allocation
After 23.456 days: claim 23.456/365ths of your allocation
After any amount of time: claim the proportional amount vested
Total vesting period: 16 months from distributor start (~485 days total - all tokens fully vested)
Grace period: Days 486-610 (~4 additional months) provide extra time to claim your fully-vested tokens
Claim deadline: 20 months after distributor start (~610 days), all unclaimed tokens return to DAO treasury
Evolution from Airdrop 1: Airdrop 1 had 12-month linear vesting with no cliff. Airdrop 2 adds a 4-month cliff period to further encourage long-term commitment and reduce immediate selling pressure.
