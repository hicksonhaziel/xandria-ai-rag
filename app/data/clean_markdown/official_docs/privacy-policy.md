## Introduction to Xandeum Foundation Privacy Policy
Xandeum Foundation collects, uses, and discloses information about you when you use the Website, contact customer service, engage with us on social media, or otherwise interact with us.

## Collection of Information
We collect information you provide directly to us, such as:
* Name
* Email address
* Postal address
* Phone number
* Credit card and other payment information
* Any other information you choose to provide

We also collect information automatically when you interact with us, including:
* Transactional information
* Device and usage information
* Location information
* Information collected by cookies and similar tracking technologies

## Use of Information
We use the information we collect to:
* Provide, maintain, and improve our products and services
* Process transactions and send related information
* Personalize and improve your experience on our Website
* Send technical notices, security alerts, and support and administrative messages
* Respond to your comments and questions and provide customer service
* Communicate with you about products, services, and events
* Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our Website
* Facilitate contests, sweepstakes, and promotions
* Detect, investigate, and prevent security incidents and other malicious activity
* Debug to identify and repair errors in our Website
* Comply with our legal and financial obligations

## Sharing of Information
We share personal information in the following circumstances:
* With vendors, service providers, and consultants that need access to personal information
* If we believe that disclosure is in accordance with, or required by, any applicable law or legal process
* If we believe that your actions are inconsistent with our user agreements or policies
* With our lawyers and other professional advisors where necessary to obtain advice or protect our business interests
* In connection with, or during negotiations concerning, any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business
* With your consent or at your direction

## Analytics
We allow others to provide analytics services on our behalf across the web and in mobile apps. These entities may use cookies, web beacons, device identifiers, and other technologies to collect information about your use of our Website and other websites and applications.

## Transfer of Information to the United States and Other Countries
Xandeum operates and engages service providers in various jurisdictions. Therefore, we and our service providers may transfer your personal information to, or store or access it in, jurisdictions that may not provide levels of data protection that are equivalent to those of your home jurisdiction.

## Your Choices
* Cookies: Most browsers are set to accept cookies by default. You can usually set your browser to disable cookies or alert you when cookies are being sent.
* Communications Preferences: You may opt out of receiving newsletters from Xandeum by following the instructions in those communications.

## Your California Privacy Rights
If you are a California resident, you have certain rights with respect to your personal information, including the right to request to know more about the categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect, use, and disclose.

## Additional Disclosures for Individuals in Europe
If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have certain rights and protections under the law regarding the processing of your personal data. You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you and to receive your data in a portable format.
