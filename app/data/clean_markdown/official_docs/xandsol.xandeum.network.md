## Introduction to Xandeum LST
Xandeum LST is a liquid staking platform that allows users to stake their SOL and earn rewards. 

## Getting Started
To get started, users need to:
1. Connect their Solana wallet
2. Stake their SOL to get xandSOL and become part of the innovative liquid staking pool
3. Earn yield by contributing to the network's security

* Block Rewards

* Future Storage Rewards (Coming Soon)
