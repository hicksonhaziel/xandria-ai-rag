## Introduction to Xandeum Validators
Xandeum is offering a unique opportunity for validators to join our innovative storage-enabled liquid staking pool.

## Why Join Xandeum?
* Be a Pioneer: Be among the first validators to support Xandeum's groundbreaking storage solution.
* Early Adopter Rewards: Enjoy a larger share of the pool due to limited initial validators.
* Future-Proof Your Staking: Join a pool that's built for the future, with advanced features and a focus on innovation.
* Customized MEV Rewards: Set your own MEV percentage to share with stakers from our pool.
* Flexible Block Reward Sharing: Choose how much of your block rewards to allocate to stakers.

## How to Join
1. Adopt Xandeum-Solana Validator Software: Commit to using our specialized validator software when it becomes available in 2025.
2. Set Up a Bond Account: Create a bond account with sufficient collateral to participate in the pool.
