## Introduction to the Blockchain Trilemma
The Challenge: The Storage Trilemma

Storing massive amounts of data on a blockchain is a huge challenge. Xandeum is tackling a specific Storage Trilemma that holds back decentralized technology: smart contract native, scalable to exabytes, and random access. Most solutions can’t deliver all three, forcing trade-offs that limit blockchain’s potential.

## Requirements to Solve the Storage Trilemma
To solve the storage Trilemma, the following requirements must be met:
* Smart Contract Native: Storage must integrate seamlessly with smart contracts to enable dynamic, programmable applications directly on the blockchain.
* Scalable to Exabytes: Blockchain storage needs to handle massive datasets—think billions of gigabytes—without slowing down or becoming unaffordable.
* Random Access: Users and apps need to quickly retrieve specific data without sifting through entire datasets, making storage practical for real-world use.

## Xandeum’s Solution: A Revolutionary Storage Layer
Xandeum is building a groundbreaking storage layer that solves the Storage Trilemma by integrating directly with Solana, one of the fastest blockchains. Our innovative approach ensures storage is smart contract native, scalable to exabytes, and supports random access.

### Key Components of Xandeum’s Solution
* Smart Contract Native: Xandeum seamlessly integrates a scalable storage layer into Solana, enabling smart contracts to store and manage data directly on-chain.
* Scalable to Exabytes+: Xandeum’s network of storage provider nodes (pNodes) stores data in pages with configurable redundancy levels, ensuring billions of gigabytes can be handled efficiently.
* Random Access: Xandeum’s Peek (read) and Poke (write) operations allow developers to quickly retrieve or update specific data between Solana accounts and our storage layer.
