## XAND Stake Portal
The XAND Stake Portal is available at https://stakexand.xandeum.network.

## Staking XAND Tokens
To stake your XAND token in the XAND DAO for a 16% APY, follow these steps:
1. Go to the DAO and connect your wallet.
2. Deposit your XAND.
3. Click "Lock your tokens".
4. Choose "Constant".
5. For duration, click on "Custom".

> IMPORTANT: Follow the above steps carefully to ensure proper staking configuration.
